Key Canadian Freight Lanes

  • Toronto (GTA) ⇄ Montreal – Canada’s busiest freight corridor driven by retail, food, pharma, and manufacturing.
  • Toronto (GTA) ⇄ Calgary / Edmonton – Major east–west lane serving industrial and distribution freight.
  • Toronto (GTA) ⇄ Vancouver – National long-haul corridor supporting import, retail, and consumer goods.
  • Montreal ⇄ Halifax / Moncton – Atlantic regional distribution and port-connected freight.
  • GTA ⇄ Southern Ontario – High-frequency short-haul lanes for automotive and manufacturing supply chains.

We currently operate within Canada only, with plans to expand into cross-border lanes in the future.
